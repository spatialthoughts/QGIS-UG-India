## English Language Tutorials

- [QGIS Playlist](https://jaiindimple.github.io/posts/qgis-playlist/): QGIS playlist covering fundamental concepts, spatial analysis, raster and vector data handling, and plugins by Dimple Jain.
- [QGIS Playlist](https://www.qgistutorials.com/en/): Step-by-step tutorials covering basic, intermediate, and advanced QGIS operations by Ujaval Gandhi.
